Academic Teaching

I have been involved in the teaching, support, continuous development, and creation of undergraduate and graduate courses and technical laboratories in the following areas:

  • Finite Element Analysis
  • Dynamics
  • Instrumentation
  • Control
  • Robotics
  • Engineering Design
  • CAD Modeling
  • Thermofluids
I have also supported the creation and redesign of courses for new undergraduate and graduate programs offered at the American University of Sharjah as part of their modernized curriculum introduced in 2024. The majors were Mechanical Engineering 2.0 program, Intilligent Systems and Mechatronics Engineering program and Electric Vehicles and Mobility Engineering.
